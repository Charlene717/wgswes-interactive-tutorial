ACMG / AMP 變異分類

28 條證據規則 + 2025 點數系統：把變異化成 P / LP / VUS / LB / B 的標準框架。

28-criterion framework + 2025 point system: standardised P / LP / VUS / LB / B classification.

概覽

ACMG / AMP 2015 + 演化

Richards 2015 (Genet Med) 由 American College of Medical Genetics 與 Association for Molecular Pathology 共同制定，定義 5 級分類：Pathogenic (P) / Likely Pathogenic (LP) / Variant of Uncertain Significance (VUS) / Likely Benign (LB) / Benign (B)。基於 28 條證據（16 條病原 + 12 條良性）的組合規則。

後續演化：(1) 2018 SVI splicing 規則更新；(2) Tayoun 2018 (Hum Mutat) PVS1 細化決策樹（去除/重啟動 codon、剪接破壞、整體 NMD 觸發）；(3) 2025 ClinGen SVI 點數系統 — 將證據強度轉換為點數（PVS=8, PS=4, PM=2, PP=1, 良性負分），總分對應分類。

Richards 2015 (Genet Med), jointly authored by ACMG & AMP, defines 5 tiers: Pathogenic (P) / Likely Pathogenic (LP) / Variant of Uncertain Significance (VUS) / Likely Benign (LB) / Benign (B). Built on 28 criteria (16 pathogenic + 12 benign) combined by rules.

Evolution: (1) 2018 SVI splicing updates; (2) Tayoun 2018 (Hum Mutat) PVS1 refinement tree (loss vs alternate start, splice disruption, NMD); (3) 2025 ClinGen SVI point system — points per evidence (PVS=8, PS=4, PM=2, PP=1; benign negative), summed for classification.

💡
2025 點數系統優勢：解決了「PM2_supporting + PP3 是否能升 LP」這種長期模糊地帶。Pejaver 2022、Tavtigian 2018 (GIM) 用 Bayesian model 推導出點數對應—— P ≥ 10 分、LP 6-9 分、VUS 0-5 分、LB -1 to -6、B ≤ -7。 2025 point-system advantage: resolves long-standing ambiguity (e.g. "does PM2_supporting + PP3 reach LP?"). Pejaver 2022, Tavtigian 2018 (GIM) derived points via Bayesian model — P ≥ 10, LP 6–9, VUS 0–5, LB -1 to -6, B ≤ -7.

⚠️
BA1 陷阱：原始 5% 為通用閾值，但 ClinGen 已針對特定疾病/基因下調（例如 BRCA1/2 用 ≥ 1%）。勿盲套 5%——必須查 ClinGen gene-specific specification。亦不可僅憑 BA1 認定 B：需排除創始人效應（founder effect）。 BA1 pitfall: default 5% is generic, but ClinGen specifies lower thresholds per gene (e.g. BRCA1/2 uses ≥1%). Don't apply 5% blindly — consult ClinGen gene-specific specifications. BA1 alone is insufficient if a founder effect could explain high MAF.
