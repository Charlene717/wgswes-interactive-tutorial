STEP 6 / 16

覆蓋率與 QC 指標

「30× WGS」到底是什麼意思？均勻性、Lorenz/Gini、WES bait 效率與臨床 ≥20× 門檻。

What does "30× WGS" really mean? Uniformity, Lorenz/Gini, WES bait efficiency and the clinical ≥20× threshold.

概覽

為什麼平均深度不夠用？

業界常說「30× WGS」，但這個 30× 究竟是平均 (mean) 還是中位數 (median)？兩者在偏態分佈下差異巨大。少數高覆蓋區 (rDNA、粒線體、衛星) 能把 mean 拉高很多，但 median 與「目標區能否呼叫變異」更相關。

真正關鍵的是均勻性 (uniformity)：有沒有夠多 base 達到呼叫所需深度？臨床標準通常要求目標區 ≥20× 覆蓋率 > 95–98%。

People say "30× WGS" — but is that mean or median? In a skewed distribution they differ greatly. A few hyper-covered regions (rDNA, mtDNA, satellites) inflate the mean; median better reflects "can we call here?"

The real key is uniformity: how much of the target region reaches callable depth? Clinical reporting typically requires >95–98% of target at ≥20×.

💡
核心工具：Picard CollectWgsMetrics / CollectHsMetrics (WES 用)、mosdepth (Pedersen 2018) 快速 per-region 深度、Qualimap (Okonechnikov 2016) 一站式 alignment QC。最後用 MultiQC 整合。

 Core tools: Picard CollectWgsMetrics / CollectHsMetrics (WES), mosdepth (Pedersen 2018) for fast per-region depth, Qualimap (Okonechnikov 2016) for one-stop alignment QC. Aggregate with MultiQC.
核心指標

一、必看的 6 個覆蓋率指標

📏

Mean / Median depth

WGS 報 mean；中位數較不受極端值影響。理想 mean ≈ median，偏離大代表分佈不均。

WGS reports mean; median is robust to extremes. Ideally mean ≈ median; large gap = uneven coverage.

📈

% Bases ≥ 20×

臨床報告核心指標：目標區內達 20× 的比例。WES 應 >95%；WGS 30× 通常 >90%。

Clinical workhorse: fraction of target ≥20×. WES expected >95%; 30× WGS usually >90%.

⚖️

Gini coefficient

Lorenz 曲線下面積。0 = 完全均勻；越高越不均。WGS 良好 < 0.20。

Area under the Lorenz curve. 0 = perfectly uniform; higher = less uniform. Good WGS < 0.20.

🎯

On-bait %

WES 限定：reads 落在 bait 區的比例。良好 >60–70%；< 50% 表示 capture 效率差。

WES only: fraction of reads on bait. Good >60–70%; < 50% means poor capture.

🌀

Fold-80 base penalty

Picard 指標：將 80% base 拉到 mean 需要的倍數。1 = 完美均勻；越大越不均。

Picard metric: fold-coverage needed to bring 80% of bases to mean. 1 = perfect; higher = uneven.

🔬

GC bias / AT-dropout

GC <30% 或 >65% 區域深度下降。AT-rich exon (例：HBB 啟動子) 為常見盲區。

Depth drops at GC <30% or >65%. AT-rich exons (e.g. HBB promoter) are common blind spots.

⚠️
WES 陷阱：capture kit 廠商之間 bait 設計差異大 (Twist vs Agilent vs IDT xGen)。比較不同 kit 的「mean coverage」毫無意義，應比較同一 target interval 內的 ≥20× %。 WES pitfall: bait designs vary substantially across vendors (Twist vs Agilent vs IDT xGen). Comparing raw "mean coverage" across kits is meaningless — compare %≥20× over a shared target interval.
互動模擬

深度分佈與均勻性

調整 mean depth 與分佈偏度，觀察 % ≥20× 與 Gini 變化。即使 mean 很高，若分佈尾巴重，仍會有大量低覆蓋盲區。

Tune mean depth and dispersion to see how % ≥20× and Gini change. Even with high mean, heavy-tailed distributions leave many blind spots.

程式碼

覆蓋率工具實作

# WGS coverage with CollectWgsMetrics
gatk CollectWgsMetrics \
    -I bam/S1.recal.bam -R ref/GRCh38.fa \
    -O qc/S1.wgs_metrics.txt \
    --MINIMUM_BASE_QUALITY 20 \
    --MINIMUM_MAPPING_QUALITY 20
# WES with CollectHsMetrics — needs bait & target intervals
gatk BedToIntervalList \
    -I capture/twist_baits.bed -SD ref/GRCh38.dict \
    -O capture/baits.interval_list

gatk CollectHsMetrics \
    -I bam/S1.recal.bam -R ref/GRCh38.fa \
    -BI capture/baits.interval_list \
    -TI capture/targets.interval_list \
    -O qc/S1.hs_metrics.txt \
    --COVERAGE_CAP 1000
# mosdepth — fast per-region depth (Pedersen 2018)
mosdepth --threads 4 --by capture/targets.bed \
    --thresholds 1,10,20,30,50 \
    --no-per-base \
    qc/S1 bam/S1.recal.bam

# Aggregate everything with MultiQC
multiqc qc/ -o qc/multiqc
