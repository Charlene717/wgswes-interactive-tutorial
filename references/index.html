補充說明 · Notes

GATK 4.x「Best Practices」一名已成歷史包袱

教學若預設「GATK4 Best Practices = 最佳實踐」需更新：自 2020 起 PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge V2 (Olson et al. 2022, Cell Genomics) 起，DRAGEN-GATK（Illumina+Broad 合作版）與 DeepVariant 在 F1、indel sensitivity、difficult region (low-mappability / homopolymer) 皆系統性超越 default GATK4 HaplotypeCaller+VQSR。Sentieon DNAseq 雖數學等價於 GATK 但速度顯著加快，常用於商業臨床 pipeline。實務原則：(1) 教學應 同時介紹 GATK4 + DeepVariant + DRAGEN-GATK 三條主流線，而非單推 GATK4；(2) VQSR 在小樣本 (n < 30 WGS or n < 1 WES) 無法穩定訓練，需改用 hard filter 或 CNNScoreVariants；(3) 報告必須列出 caller 版本、reference build (GRCh38 vs T2T-CHM13)、filter strategy；(4) GIAB HG002 truth set 為公認 benchmark baseline。

If the tutorial defaults to 'GATK4 Best Practices = gold standard', update: since the 2020+ PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge V2 (Olson et al. 2022, Cell Genomics), DRAGEN-GATK (Illumina+Broad joint) and DeepVariant systematically beat default GATK4 HaplotypeCaller+VQSR on F1, indel sensitivity, and difficult regions (low-mappability / homopolymer). Sentieon DNAseq is mathematically equivalent to GATK but markedly faster — common in commercial clinical pipelines. Rules: (1) the tutorial should introduce three mainstream lines — GATK4 + DeepVariant + DRAGEN-GATK in parallel, not GATK4 alone; (2) VQSR cannot train stably on small cohorts (n < 30 WGS or n < 1 WES) — switch to hard filter or CNNScoreVariants; (3) reports must list caller version, reference build (GRCh38 vs T2T-CHM13), and filter strategy; (4) GIAB HG002 truth set is the agreed benchmark baseline.

Sources: Olson ND et al. (2022) PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge V2, Cell Genomics 2:100129. DOI: 10.1016/j.xgen.2022.100129; Krusche P et al. (2019) Nat Biotechnol 37:555; Poplin R et al. (2018) DeepVariant, Nat Biotechnol 36:983.

補充說明 · Notes

ACMG 2023 點數系統：尚未成為臨床標準

教學若以「ACMG 2023 點數系統取代 2015 五級分類」描述，需澄清：自 Tavtigian 等 2018 年提出 Bayesian 點數模型（將 PVS/PS/PM/PP 與 BA/BS/BP 映射為 +8/+4/+2/+1 與 -8/-4/-1 點），ClinGen SVI 工作小組近年發布逐項 criterion 修訂（PVS1、PP1、PP3/BP4、PM2、BS2 等），並推動 2023 點數系統取代「規則計數」。然而：(1) 截至 2026 年，2015 Richards 5-tier 仍為 ACMG 官方核心框架，臨床 lab 報告（ACGS UK 2020/2023 best practice、CAP/CLIA 認證實驗室）仍預設使用 2015 規則；(2) 點數系統在 ClinGen Variant Curation Interface (VCI) 與部分 expert panel 中作為「進階模式」可用，但 非強制；(3) VarSome / Franklin / InterVar 提供 2015 與點數雙模式。實務原則：(a) 教學應同時呈現兩種框架；(b) 報告須註明採用版本（如「ACMG/AMP 2015 with ClinGen SVI 2018–2023 refinements」）；(c) 商業平台（VarSome v11+, Franklin 2024）顯示的「P/LP」結論可能因採用不同框架而不一致，需人工 reconcile。

If the tutorial states 'the 2023 ACMG point system replaces the 2015 5-tier classification', clarify: Tavtigian et al. (2018) proposed a Bayesian point model mapping PVS/PS/PM/PP and BA/BS/BP to +8/+4/+2/+1 and -8/-4/-1; ClinGen SVI has since refined individual criteria (PVS1, PP1, PP3/BP4, PM2, BS2) and pushed a 2023 point system replacing rule-counting. However: (1) as of 2026, the 2015 Richards 5-tier remains the official ACMG core framework; ACGS UK 2020/2023 best-practice and CAP/CLIA labs default to the 2015 rules; (2) the point system is available as an 'advanced mode' in ClinGen VCI and some expert panels but is not mandated; (3) VarSome / Franklin / InterVar offer dual modes. Rules: (a) present both frameworks; (b) reports must state the version used (e.g. 'ACMG/AMP 2015 with ClinGen SVI 2018–2023 refinements'); (c) commercial platforms (VarSome v11+, Franklin 2024) may show different P/LP labels under different frameworks — manually reconcile.

Sources: Richards S et al. (2015) Genet Med 17:405. DOI: 10.1038/gim.2015.30; Tavtigian SV et al. (2018) Genet Med 20:1054. DOI: 10.1038/gim.2017.210; Pejaver V et al. (2022) AJHG 109:2163.

補充說明 · Notes

參考基因組：GRCh38 / T2T-CHM13 / 1000G+decoys 的選擇

教學在 alignment 章節若僅推「最新 T2T-CHM13」，需補充：(1) GRCh38 (hs38DH, 含 ALT + decoys + HLA) 仍為 ClinVar、gnomAD v2/v3、絕大多數臨床 panel 與 medical-grade 工具鏈的標準座標系；(2) T2T-CHM13 (Nurk 2022 Science) 新增 ~200 Mbp 序列、修正 GRCh38 數千處錯誤、揭露 segmental duplication 與 centromere 結構，但 gnomAD v4 (Chen 2024 Nature) 同時釋出 GRCh38 與 T2T；ClinVar 尚未全面 lift-over；(3) 臨床診斷至 2026 年仍建議使用 GRCh38 + lift-over 補充，研究探索可雙 build；(4) HPRC pangenome (Liao 2023 Nature) 為下一代方向，但 vg/GIRAFFE 流程尚未進入 GA4GH 認證的臨床 pipeline。

If the alignment chapter pushes only 'the newest T2T-CHM13', note: (1) GRCh38 (hs38DH with ALT + decoys + HLA) remains the standard coordinate system for ClinVar, gnomAD v2/v3, and most clinical panels / medical-grade tools; (2) T2T-CHM13 (Nurk 2022 Science) adds ~200 Mbp, fixes thousands of GRCh38 errors, and reveals segmental duplication / centromere structure — gnomAD v4 (Chen 2024 Nature) releases both builds but ClinVar has not fully lifted over; (3) for clinical diagnosis as of 2026, recommend GRCh38 + supplementary lift-over; dual-build is fine for research; (4) the HPRC pangenome (Liao 2023 Nature) is the next direction but vg/GIRAFFE has not entered GA4GH-accredited clinical pipelines.

Sources: Nurk S et al. (2022) Science 376:44; Aganezov S et al. (2022) Science 376:eabl3533; Liao WW et al. (2023) Nature 617:312.

補充說明 · Notes

BQSR：流程現代化的取捨

教學若預設「所有 caller 之前一律先跑 BQSR」需澄清：BQSR 假設 known-sites VCF 含絕大多數真實變異，將其餘 mismatch 視為 base error 而校正 Q score。然而：(1) DeepVariant 與 DRAGEN ML caller 不使用 BAM 中的 Q score 做 likelihood，而是從 read pileup pixel 學習特徵，故 跑或不跑 BQSR 對 F1 幾乎無差（PrecisionFDA v2 結果）；(2) HaplotypeCaller 仍依賴 BQSR；(3) 非人類或缺乏 known-sites 物種應跳過 BQSR，或先做 self-bootstrap。實務：(a) HaplotypeCaller 流程保留 BQSR；(b) DeepVariant / DRAGEN 流程可省略以節省時間（~30% wall-clock）；(c) 教學圖表應標示「BQSR 為 GATK-specific」。

If the tutorial states 'always run BQSR before any caller', clarify: BQSR assumes known-sites VCF covers most true variants and treats remaining mismatches as base error to recalibrate Q. However: (1) DeepVariant and DRAGEN ML caller do not use BAM Q scores in their likelihood — they learn features from read-pileup pixels, so BQSR has near-zero F1 impact (PrecisionFDA v2 result); (2) HaplotypeCaller still relies on BQSR; (3) skip BQSR for non-human species or those without known-sites VCFs, or self-bootstrap. Rules: (a) keep BQSR in HaplotypeCaller pipelines; (b) it can be skipped in DeepVariant / DRAGEN pipelines (saves ~30% wall-clock); (c) annotate tutorial diagrams 'BQSR is GATK-specific'.

Sources: Olson ND et al. (2022) PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge V2; Yun T et al. (2020) DeepVariant + GLnexus, Bioinformatics 36:5582.

WES kit：Twist / IDT xGen / Agilent SureSelect / Roche KAPA 的差異

教學若視 WES 為「同質」需澄清：主流 kit (Twist Exome 2.0, IDT xGen Exome Hyb v2, Agilent SureSelect XT HS2, Roche KAPA HyperExome) 在 target region 定義、capture 效率、GC bias、insert size 分布、off-target rate 均有系統性差異。Chilamakuri 2014 BMC Genomics 與後續 Shigemizu 2015、Wang 2020 等 benchmark 顯示：(1) Twist 平均 on-target 較均勻；(2) IDT xGen 對 GC-extreme region 表現較好；(3) 不同 kit 的 BED 範圍差異可達 5–10%，直接影響 CNV/SV 工具的 baseline (CNVkit / GATK gCNV 都需 panel-of-normals)。實務：(a) 報告必須註明 kit 版本與廠商；(b) BED 設定需與 kit 一致；(c) cross-kit pooling 需重新計算 PoN；(d) WES CNV 偵測對 kit 一致性極敏感。

If the tutorial treats WES as 'homogeneous', clarify: mainstream kits (Twist Exome 2.0, IDT xGen Exome Hyb v2, Agilent SureSelect XT HS2, Roche KAPA HyperExome) differ systematically in target region definition, capture efficiency, GC bias, insert size, off-target rate. Chilamakuri 2014 BMC Genomics and follow-on benchmarks show: (1) Twist has more uniform on-target depth; (2) IDT xGen handles GC-extreme regions better; (3) BED differences across kits reach 5–10%, directly affecting CNV/SV baselines (CNVkit / GATK gCNV both need panels of normals). Rules: (a) reports must state kit version + vendor; (b) BED must match the kit; (c) cross-kit pooling requires recomputing the PoN; (d) WES CNV detection is highly kit-sensitive.

Sources: Chilamakuri CSR et al. (2014) Performance comparison of four exome capture systems, BMC Genomics 15:449. DOI: 10.1186/1471-2164-15-449; Babadi M et al. (2023) GATK-gCNV, Nat Genet 55:1589.

MANE Select：報告應使用單一規範轉錄本

教學若示範 HGVS 而沒指定轉錄本，需澄清：同一變異在 RefSeq、Ensembl、UCSC 不同轉錄本中 c. 命名常不同，是診所一致性問題的主因。MANE Select (Morales 2022 Nature) 由 NCBI + EMBL-EBI 共同維護，為每個 protein-coding gene 指定 單一 RefSeq + Ensembl 等價轉錄本；MANE Plus Clinical 補充臨床重要 isoform。實務：(1) 所有臨床報告以 MANE Select 為主轉錄本；(2) VEP、SnpEff、ANNOVAR 都支援 MANE 過濾；(3) ClinVar、ClinGen 自 2023 起預設使用 MANE 命名；(4) 教學表格應同時列出 MANE Select 與 legacy refseq (NM_xxx) 以利對照。

If the tutorial shows HGVS without specifying a transcript, clarify: the same variant has different c. naming across RefSeq, Ensembl, UCSC transcripts — a leading cause of clinical inconsistency. MANE Select (Morales 2022 Nature), jointly maintained by NCBI + EMBL-EBI, designates a single equivalent RefSeq + Ensembl transcript per protein-coding gene; MANE Plus Clinical adds clinically important isoforms. Rules: (1) all clinical reports use MANE Select as primary; (2) VEP, SnpEff, ANNOVAR all support MANE filtering; (3) ClinVar and ClinGen default to MANE naming since 2023; (4) tutorials should list both MANE Select and the legacy RefSeq (NM_xxx) side by side.

Sources: Morales J et al. (2022) MANE, Nature 604:310. DOI: 10.1038/s41586-022-04558-8; den Dunnen JT et al. (2016) HGVS 2016 update, Hum Mutat 37:564.
